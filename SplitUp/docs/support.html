Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a plan?

Tap the Plans tab, then tap the + button to create a new plan. Add a title, date, and invite friends to join.

How do I split an expense?

Open a plan, tap + to add an expense. Choose who paid, enter the amount, and select a split type: equal, custom, or percentage.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and search by username, email, or name. Send a friend request and wait for them to accept.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data.

How do I change my username?

Go to Profile, scroll to the Edit Profile section, update your username, and tap Save Changes.
